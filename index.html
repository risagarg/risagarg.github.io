Welcome to my corner of the internet

Hi, I'm Risa, welcome to my website! I'm a computational biology major at the University of Pennsylvania, and I started this blog to share my thoughts on science, biotech, deeptech, and general projects I'm interested in or working on.

I'm working on research targeting intrinsically disordered proteins, proteins that don't have a stable 3D structure, and I spent the past year spinning out my research into a startup. I've also worked on genetically engineering oncolytic viruses to selectively target cancer cells at Humane Genomics, and I love taking on small coding projects related to biology.

In my free time, I love to read, write (I share my thoughts on X, and right here on my blog) and play music (guitar, piano, vocals).
